Overview

Cadbury is a renowned British brand of chocolate that has been crafting delightful chocolates for over a century. Known for its rich and creamy taste, Cadbury offers a wide range of chocolate varieties that cater to diverse palates.

History

Founded by John Cadbury in 1824, Cadbury has a fascinating history. Initially started as a small business in Birmingham, England, Cadbury's commitment to quality and innovation has made it a household name worldwide.

Cadbury Crispy Crunch is a delightful chocolate variety that offers a unique blend of smooth milk chocolate and crispy, crunchy goodness. Fun Facts

  • Cadbury Dairy Milk was introduced in 1905 and has become one of the world's most popular chocolate bars.
  • The iconic Cadbury Dairy Milk packaging color, purple, was introduced in 1914.
  • Cadbury produces more than 350 million pounds of chocolate each year.
